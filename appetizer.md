# Garlic Bread

## Ingredients
- 1 baguette
- 4 tbsp butter (softened)
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- Salt
- Optional: parsley

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
2. Mix butter, garlic, and salt
3. Spread mixture on sliced bread
4. Bake for 10–12 minutes
